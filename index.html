Portfolio

The National Parks Discover-E-Pass

Inspired by the Passport to your National Parks, the National Parks Discover-E-Pass is a program to "stamp" every national park, monument, and historic site that you have ever attended or wish to attend. I built this because I own a National Parks Passport, I wanted to pay tribute to those who work to hard to preserve the 490+ environmental and historic wonders which make up the National Parks System. The Naitonal Parks Discover-E-Pass is a program that aims to inspire others to explore the many national parks and hopefully visit them all. It is a fullstack program made with React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and CSS. Please feel free to view the client code here and the server code here

Bearfoot: Your Guide to the Outdoors

Bearfoot is a program designed to find hiking trails near you. Just enter your address, town, zip code, or coordiantes to explore different green spaces anywhere in the United States. I made Bearfoot because, wherever you are in the United States, you are always close to green space or to the great outdoors, and this program is perfect for those who love hiking and the outdoors as much as I do. This program was made with Javascript, jQuery, Restful APIs, HTML5, and CSS. You can find view the code here here
