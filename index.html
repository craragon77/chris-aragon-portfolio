The National Parks Discover-E-Pass

Inspired by the Passport to your National Parks, the National Parks Discover-E-Pass is a program to "stamp" every national park, monument, and historic site that you have ever attended or wish to attend. It is a fullstack program made with React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, and CSS. Please feel free to view the client code here and the server code here

Bearfoot: Your Guide to the Outdoors

Bearfoot is a program designed to find hiking trails near you. Just enter your address, town, zip code, or coordiantes to explore different green spaces anywhere in the United States. This program was made with Javascript, Restful APIs, HTML5, and CSS. You can find view the code here here
